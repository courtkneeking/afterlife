About site
A website created to make it easier to connect with friends and relatives of your loved one after their passing. Includes features such as an obituary template, a slideshow generator, gofundme widget and much more. To get a better idea, take a look at the example page here. This site will always be free and the pages will never be deleted unless done so by the page's creator. Register now to make a memorial webpage for your loved one. Sorry for your loss.
